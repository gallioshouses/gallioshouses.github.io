Αffordable Luxury

Afroditi Suite is a calm, modern and luxurious place to stay on your vacation on a complex of houses outside of Rethymno city. You can enjoy some calm moments on the hot tub or have an enjoyable BBQ night. The suite offers a convenient living room to relax or watch a movie on the TV. It is located 15 minutes from Rethymnon Centre by car which you can park on the parking lot of the apartment.

In a complex of houses in Loutra, Rethymno, in a beautifully landscaped area with a garden and swimming pools. Its a well-decorated first floor house with a Hot Tub on the beautiful terrace. Independent entrance is available.

Guests will have access and full privacy to all areas indoor and outdoor!
